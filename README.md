# Supply Chain Management Dashboard

This project features a comprehensive analytical dashboard designed to track supply chain performance, sales metrics, and inventory status. It provides a high-level overview of sales trends, regional distribution, and year-over-year growth.

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze supply chain sales, inventory, and regional performance to help businesses make data-driven decisions and improve efficiency.

## ðŸ”Ž Key Insights & Visualizations

The dashboard is divided into several analytical sections:

### 1. Product & Growth Analysis
* **Total Sales by Product Type:** Breakdowns of revenue by category. "Arts & Entertainment" leads with **23.38%**, followed closely by "Photography" and "Music".
* **YOY Growth %:** Tracks annual growth rates.
    * *Trend:* Strong growth in 2020 (16%), followed by a slowdown in 2021-2022, and a significant projected drop in 2023 (-73%).

### 2. Regional & Store Performance
* **Total Sales by Region:** The **West** region is the top performer (112M), followed by the South (99M). The Southwest region trails at 45M.
* **Top 5 Sales by State:** **California** dominates sales (96M), significantly outperforming the second-highest state, Florida (33M).
* **Top 5 Store Wise Sales:** A granular look at top-performing individual store locations, led by the Tilloch Store (6.2M).

### 3. Time Series Analysis
* **Monthly Sales Trend:** A line graph displaying seasonal fluctuations. Sales peak significantly in **March (41M)** and dip towards November.
* **YTD, QTD, MTD Sales:** A complex bar chart visualizing sales performance across Quarters and Months for detailed historical comparison (2019-2021).
